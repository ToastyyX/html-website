My Favorite Band: Cocteau Twins

Origin:

  • The Cocteau Twins were a Scottish alternative rock band formed in the early 1980s. Known for their dreamy and ethereal soundscapes, they became pioneers of the shoegaze and dream pop genres. The band's original lineup consisted of vocalist Elizabeth Fraser, guitarist Robin Guthrie, and bassist Will Heggie. Heggie was later replaced by Simon Raymonde, who became an integral part of the band's distinctive sound.

      

Discography:

  • Cocteau Twins have released numerous tracks throughout the years spamming from the early 1980s to late 1990s. Throughout their career, the Cocteau Twins released a series of critically acclaimed albums, including "Treasure" (1984), "Heaven or Las Vegas" (1990), and "Blue Bell Knoll" (1988), among many others. They've released 10 studio albums beginning with "Garlands" (1982) and concluding with "Milk & Kisses" (1996).
